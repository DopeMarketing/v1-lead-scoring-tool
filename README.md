# V1 - Lead Scoring Tool

## Overview
An automated lead scoring system that evaluates prospects from multiple marketing channels based on geographic location, contact validation, and request intent to prioritize high-value leads for sales follow-up. The tool integrates with Meta Ads, Google Ads, HubSpot, and Gmail to automatically score and rank incoming leads.

## Problem
Sales teams waste time on low-quality leads while high-value prospects get buried in the flood of inquiries, leading to missed opportunities and inefficient resource allocation

## Target User
Small business owners and sales teams managing high volumes of leads from multiple digital marketing channels

## Features
- Meta Ads lead capture integration with automatic scoring
- Geographic location scoring with customizable territory weighting
- Email validation and phone number verification scoring
- Keyword-based request categorization and intent scoring
- Lead quality dashboard with filtering and prioritization
